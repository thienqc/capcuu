# HẠ CANXI MÁU
[Clinical manifestations of hypocalcemia - UpToDate](https://www.uptodate.com/contents/clinical-manifestations-of-hypocalcemia)

## Biểu hiện
**Acute**
- Neuromuscular irritability (tetany)
	- Paresthesias (perioral, extremities)
	- Muscle twitching
	- Carpopedal spasm
	- Trousseau's sign
	- Chvostek's sign
	- Seizures
	- Laryngospasm
	- Bronchospasm
- Cardiac
	- Prolonged QT interval
	- Hypotension
	- Heart failure
	- Arrhythmia
	- Papilledema
**Chronic**
- Ectopic calcification (basal ganglia)
- Extrapyramidal signs
- Parkinsonism
- Dementia
- Subcapsular cataracts
- Abnormal dentition
- Dry skin
