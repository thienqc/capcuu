# Heparin

MOA:: binds to antithrombin III thereby potentiating inactivation of thrombin and factors IX, Xa, XI, XII; prevents fibrinogen â†’ fibrin; preferential inactivation of thrombin over other clotting factors

Dose:: (1) Venous thromboembolism: 80 units/kg IV x 1, then 18 units/kg/hour; (2) ACS or Afib: 60 units/kg IV x 1, then 12 units/kg/hr
